Frequently Asked Questions

How is runway calculated?

Runway = Cash √∑ Net Monthly Burn. We project forward month-by-month, accounting for revenue growth and expense increases, until cash hits zero or you become profitable.

What is "Default Dead"?

A term coined by Paul Graham. If your current trajectory leads to running out of money before becoming profitable (and you're not growing fast enough to raise), you're "default dead." If you'll survive, you're "default alive."

How much runway should I have?

Most VCs recommend 18-24 months of runway. Less than 6 months is dangerous territory ‚Äî start fundraising or cutting costs immediately.

Is my data saved anywhere?
