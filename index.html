Frequently Asked Questions

How is runway calculated?

Runway = Cash / Net Burn Rate. We also factor in revenue growth and expense changes to give you a more accurate projection over time.

What's a healthy runway?

Most VCs recommend 12-18 months of runway. Less than 6 months is danger zone. 18+ months gives you negotiating power.

Is my data safe?

Yes! All calculations happen in your browser. We never send your financial data to any server. Your numbers stay private.

How do I reduce my burn rate?

Focus on: 1) Cut non-essential expenses, 2) Renegotiate contracts, 3) Delay hiring, 4) Increase revenue per customer.

When should I start fundraising?

Start when you have 6-9 months of runway left. Fundraising typically takes 3-6 months, so you need buffer.

What's a good burn multiple?

Burn multiple = Net Burn / Net New ARR. Below 1.5x is great, 1.5-2x is good, above 2x means you're burning too much per dollar of growth.
